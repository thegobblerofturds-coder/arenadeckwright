TURDGOBBLER'S DECK NAME COLOURIFIER — VERSION 5

Open index.html in a modern browser. No installation, account, or internet connection is required.

Version 5 is the single-screen command-deck build:
- direct gradient editing with two to seven positioned stops
- editable three- or six-digit hex codes beside every colour swatch
- draggable internal stop sliders with touch, mouse, and keyboard control
- lock individual colours while trying MTG identities or ready-made palettes
- tap the gradient tube to insert an interpolated stop at that exact point
- compact left, right, remove, rotate, flip, and undo controls
- ordered W/U/B/R/G composition with numbered mana selections and automatic identity names
- dormant MTG guild and three-colour preset rail revealed by the selected mana pips
- a labelled, full-size palette-library control beside the style pad
- a single open/close palette toggle with no redundant tray controls
- built-in palettes plus four deliberate local save slots
- locally remembered gradient and formatting choices; deck names are never stored
- global bold, italic, underline, and strikethrough controls
- automatic gradient-stage reduction around Arena's 64-character raw limit
- exact over-limit reporting while retaining copy access
- Ctrl+Enter / Command+Enter copying and Ctrl+Z / Command+Z gradient undo
- reinforced in-button copy confirmation alongside the floating confirmation
- selected-stop pulses that connect each stop card to its gradient marker
- larger coarse-pointer touch targets for compact mobile controls
- stronger supported-device haptic feedback
- a unified smoked-glass cyberpunk instrument finish with palette-reactive backlighting and reduced-motion support

Everything runs locally in the browser. Nothing is uploaded.
